Privacy Policy for Seasonal App

Last updated: December 20, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to Seasonal. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle any information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Our app collects and uses the following information:

  • Local Storage Data: We store your preferences and seasonal produce data locally on your device using SQLite database (sqflite).
  • Cache Data: Images of produce are cached locally on your device to improve performance and reduce data usage.
  • Network Access: The app requires internet access to:
    • Download and update produce images
    • Load Google Fonts for better typography

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To improve user experience by caching images and fonts
  • To store your preferences locally

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal information to external servers except for:

  • Downloading images through cached_network_image package
  • Loading Google Fonts

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your local data through the app
  • Clear cached images and local storage through app settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts API for typography
  • Image caching services for produce images

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All data collection is limited to local device storage for app functionality.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
